Home/About Us

About JKUpdates.in

JKUpdates.in is an independent, student-focused news aggregator dedicated to Jammu & Kashmir's educational and public service landscape. We bring together official notifications from three key bodies:

  • Kashmir University – date sheets, results, exam schedules, Ph.D. admissions, and academic notices.
  • JKBOSE – Class 10 & 12 results, date sheets, improvement exam notices, and migration certificates.
  • JKSSB – recruitment advertisements, admit cards, answer keys, interview schedules, and selection lists.

Our Mission

Students and aspirants in Jammu & Kashmir often miss critical deadlines because notifications are scattered across multiple official portals. JKUpdates.in solves this by surfacing all important announcements in one clean, fast-loading feed — updated automatically every 10 minutes.

What We Are Not

We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Kashmir University, JKBOSE, JKSSB, or any government body. Always verify critical information directly on official websites.

Security & SSL Certificate

JKUpdates.in is served exclusively over HTTPS using a valid SSL/TLS certificate, ensuring all data between your browser and our server is encrypted. You will see the padlock icon 🔒 in your browser's address bar confirming the secure connection.

How to set up SSL for your own deployment:

  • Let's Encrypt via Certbot (free): On a VPS, run sudo certbot --nginx -d yourdomain.com. Auto-renews every 90 days.
  • Cloudflare (easiest): Point your nameservers to Cloudflare and enable "Full (strict)" SSL — free and instant.
  • Netlify / Vercel / GitHub Pages: SSL is provisioned automatically when you connect a custom domain.
  • cPanel hosting: Enable AutoSSL / Let's Encrypt from cPanel → SSL/TLS → Install SSL.

Google AdSense requires HTTPS before your application is approved. Ensure your SSL certificate is valid before submitting for review.
